Azucrina Records!


music, netlabel, activism, street party, experimental music, audio, events





Azucrina Records is the netlabel I founded collectively with 2 other friends. We exist because of the will to share with the world our sounds, ideas, experiences, and efforts, as well as to find more people, bands, artists and collaborators like us: people who want to make their own records, t-shirts, zines, content, festivals, culture.

We are proud to be part of all sorts of activism towards the support of the independent and experimental musical scene, as well as the exploration and protection of our free internet culture and informational-social network.

Its the work of bands, artists and collaborators that investigate the do it yourself and do it together that inspire us to organize musical and sonic experiments, concerts, workshops and events alike.

From our site we organize the content like texts, blogs,videos, pictures, albuns, releases and stencils. Throught internet, bar meetings or sunny afternoons get together we organize, discuss, share tasks, build the site, etc.
